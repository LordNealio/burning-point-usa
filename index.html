What is Burning Point USA?
Burning Point USA is a Black American multimedia news and culture platform — built from the ground up, by us, for us. Investigative journalism, video, podcasts, graphic storytelling, and community voices from Phoenix and across the nation.
Phoenix Fire

Human dignity survives death. Every story we tell, every investigation we run, every voice we platform operates from one principle: human beings are not sacrifices. Human beings are not commodities. Human dignity is sacred.
